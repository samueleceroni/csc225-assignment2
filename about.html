Our story

Hi, we are Oliver and Susan and we are the owners of Orto Bello. Our farm story begins in 1910 when our great-grandfather bought a little plot of land for his family, and during the years it has expanded. Our passion for agriculture was born when we were teenagers, when our grandfather used to take us with him in the fields; during these moments he taught us the secrets to take care of the crops. We both have an agricultural bachelor and we have several collaborations with this department at Bologna University in order to develop new researches about seeds quality; in addition to this, all of our products are periodically analysed in the department’s laboratory to ensure the best quality standards.

We decided to open up our shop in order to encourage all people to make their own vegetable garden; you don’t need previous knowledge, we will provide you all the information sheet attached to our small plants or seed boxes! If you need any further information, contact us, we will be happy to answer you!

Oliver and Susan
